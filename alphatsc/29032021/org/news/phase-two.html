Coronavirus (COVID-19) update

Phase Two: Slow and Steady

As the coronavirus (COVID-19) cases drop to a very low level, we can begin to reopen in person.

Published 16 June 2020
From: Office of the Secretary and Jay Bragg

Pursuant to the changes in the coronavirus legislation, Tonbridge Spiritualist Centre will be beginning to re-open when the Hadlow Parish Council gives the approval to access and use the hall.

How it will work

Because the centre will be working to a capacity, it will be required to implement attendance groups. These groups will be created based on registration of interest on this page which will be published when reopening is announced and we will work to put you together with friends. These groups will be under capacity to allow for extra people to join should they wish later on.

At first, our risk assessment notes removing staple parts of our service such as refreshments and raffle, just to make sure our services work in principle. We will bring these back in phase three.

We will be audio recording your readings for your convenience, these will be held in accordance with our Privacy Policy, available here and published the following day with a URL you will be provided (guidance on these will be published later).

What You Will Need To Do

  • To have signed up and received an attendance group from us
  • A valid email address to receive the calendar with dates you are visiting
  • Complete the Track and Trace form which will be available online.
