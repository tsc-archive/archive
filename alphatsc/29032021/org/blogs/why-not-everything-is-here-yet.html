Why not everything is on tonbridgespiritualist.com yet.

You might notice in the first weeks of version 2, things are missing. The Web Team explain why.

Published 8 August 2020
From: Digital Service

tonbridgespiritualist.com is a new build from scratch, and you may notice some things that used to exist, don't right now.

When we began developing this site on July 30th, we wanted to put effort in to make everything perfect. That's why at launch not everything will be there.

What isn't available straight away:

  • Executive Committee
  • Calendar
  • Digital Service (full microsite)
On pages where it had existed before, you may be able to reach an archived version of the old page to find the information you need.

When these things do launch, which will be before December, they will be perfected products. Offering you all you need, because we are putting information first. That's a promise.
