To our people during coronavirus (COVID-19)

A letter from the chair Lisa Bragg on life during coronavirus (COVID-19).

Published 9 April 2020
From: Lisa Bragg

Hi all,

I am going to write down and talk to you about what is going on in terms of our family and the centre during this difficult and unprecedented time.

We are all doing alright, all going slightly insane from being confined to the four walls and back garden of our home – especially when Jay’s making some extravagant meal in the kitchen, stinking up the place with his garlic and rosemary.

We are still here for you if you need to talk, especially now, when you’re stuck at home being granted freedom to pop to the shops or go for a bit of exercise. Savour them moments, because that tiny bit of freedom builds hope that it will get better, not straight away but there are better times coming. Go sit on your front porch and have a chat to your neighbours – take it a day at a time.

Believe me when I say we are working hard to find an alternative way to bring the centre into your routine, we are looking at providing any form of medium connection, even if it means some form of Medium Tech Support or a simple tarot reading – Jay will be writing about this in due course as arrangements are made and put into place. While this will most certainly not be normal for any of us, this outbreak is allowing us to develop stronger contingencies for the future (bad weather etc.) – if any mediums are reading this and you do want to try and help us by offering yourself – message myself or Jay because we can talk through our proposals and see what you think.

I’ve commissioned Jay to make a small video just of the people of our centre – which we can use to not only remind us of who is behind us at this time but also share the family values and feel of our centre. – We are considering making an extended version of this video in the future – so if you would like to join us in the next one, keep your eyes on Facebook.

We will be keeping this website – tonbridgespiritualist.com, our Facebook and our Instagram updated with things for you to read, to keep you distracted at this hard time.

In love and light,
Lisa x
